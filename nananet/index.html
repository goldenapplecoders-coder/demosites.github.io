About Our Company

Our History

Nananet Tech Solutions was founded with a vision to bridge the digital divide in Uganda and neighboring regions. Starting as a small WiFi installation service in Nansana, Gganda, Wakiso, we've grown into a comprehensive technology solutions provider.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to include web development, captive portal solutions, and a wide range of network supplies. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a trusted technology partner for businesses and individuals alike.

Today, we serve clients across Uganda and internationally, helping them leverage technology to grow their businesses and improve connectivity in their communities.
